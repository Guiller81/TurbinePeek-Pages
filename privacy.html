TurbinePeek Privacy Policy

Effective date: March 17, 2026

This Privacy Policy explains what information TurbinePeek may collect and how that information is used when you use the app.

TurbinePeek is a field troubleshooting assistant intended for professional use. By using the app, you understand that certain information may be processed so the app can provide troubleshooting features, save session progress, and support normal app operation.

1. Information We Collect

Depending on how you use the app, TurbinePeek may collect the following information:

  • User-entered identifiers: such as a company UI entered during first-time setup and a nickname or alias used in the app.
  • App-scoped user identifier: a locally generated user ID used to keep sessions and app activity associated with the same user within TurbinePeek.
  • Troubleshooting content: chat messages, resolution notes, feedback text, and similar user-generated content entered into the app.
  • Images: photos or images you choose to attach to troubleshooting conversations.
  • Session and functionality data: session IDs, session status, points, levels, badges, apparel selections, and similar in-app progression or gamification data.
  • Local notification usage: the app may request permission to send local reminders about open sessions. TurbinePeek does not use remote push notification tokens at this time.
TurbinePeek does not collect: precise location, coarse location, advertising identifiers, cross-app tracking data, contacts, phone numbers, or payment information through the app.

2. How We Use Information

Information collected through the app may be used to:

  • provide troubleshooting and session-management functionality;
  • process chat messages, notes, feedback, and optional images submitted through the app;
  • store and manage active, resolved, or historical troubleshooting sessions;
  • support in-app features such as statistics, levels, badges, and other gamification elements;
  • review user feedback to maintain the reliability and functionality of the service;
  • maintain, troubleshoot, and secure the app.

3. AI and App Processing

TurbinePeek uses backend and AI-supported processing to help generate troubleshooting responses and related app functionality.

When you submit messages, notes, feedback, or optional photos through the app, that content may be processed so the app can provide troubleshooting responses, session management, and related functionality.

Users are informed in the app that conversations and related content may be collected and used strictly to provide troubleshooting responses, session management, and AI-supported functionality.

4. How Information May Be Handled

Information submitted through TurbinePeek may be used within the systems and workflows that support the app and its troubleshooting functionality.

Information may also be reviewed when reasonably needed to keep the app working properly, address technical issues, maintain security, or meet applicable requirements.

TurbinePeek does not use collected data for third-party advertising or cross-app tracking, and does not sell personal information through the app.

5. Data Retention

Information may be kept for as long as reasonably needed to operate the app, support troubleshooting workflows, maintain records, and keep the service reliable.

Retention may vary depending on the type of information and operational needs.

6. Security

Reasonable measures are used to help protect information processed through the app. However, no method of electronic storage or transmission is completely secure.

7. Your Choices

  • Photos: you choose whether to attach images to a troubleshooting conversation.
  • Nickname/Alias: you choose the nickname or alias entered into the app.
  • Notifications: you can manage local notification permissions in your device settings.

If you would like to request access, correction, or deletion of information associated with your use of TurbinePeek, contact guillermo.lozano@rwe.com. Requests will be reviewed in line with applicable requirements and operational needs.

8. Children’s Privacy

TurbinePeek is intended for professional users and is not designed for children.

9. Changes to This Privacy Policy

This Privacy Policy may be updated from time to time. If it changes, the effective date above will be updated.

10. Contact

If you have questions about this Privacy Policy or TurbinePeek’s data handling, contact guillermo.lozano@rwe.com.
