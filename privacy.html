TurbinePeek Privacy Policy

Effective date: March 17, 2026

This Privacy Policy explains how RWE Americas collects, uses, and shares information when you use the TurbinePeek mobile application ("TurbinePeek" or the "App").

TurbinePeek is a field troubleshooting assistant intended for professional use by authorized personnel. By using the App, you acknowledge that certain information may be processed to provide troubleshooting functionality, support in-app features, and maintain the App.

1. Information We Collect

Depending on how you use the App, TurbinePeek may collect the following information:

  • User-entered identifiers: such as a company UI entered during first-time setup and a nickname or alias used in the App.
  • App-scoped user identifier: a locally generated user ID used to associate sessions and app activity with the same user within TurbinePeek.
  • Troubleshooting content: chat messages, resolution notes, feedback text, and similar user-generated content entered into the App.
  • Images: photos or images you choose to attach to troubleshooting conversations.
  • Session and functionality data: session IDs, session status, points, levels, badges, apparel selections, and similar in-app progression or gamification data.
  • Local notification usage: the App may request permission to send local reminders about open sessions. TurbinePeek does not use remote push notification tokens at this time.
TurbinePeek does not collect: precise location, coarse location, advertising identifiers, cross-app tracking data, contacts, phone numbers, or payment information through the App.

2. How We Use Information

We use collected information to:

  • provide the App’s troubleshooting and session-management functionality;
  • send your messages and optional images to backend services that support AI-assisted troubleshooting;
  • store and manage active, resolved, or historical troubleshooting sessions;
  • support in-app features such as statistics, levels, badges, and other gamification elements;
  • review user feedback to maintain the safety, reliability, and functionality of the service;
  • maintain, troubleshoot, and secure the App and related systems.

3. AI and Backend Processing

TurbinePeek uses backend systems and AI-supported processing to help generate troubleshooting responses and related functionality. When you submit messages, notes, feedback, or optional photos through the App, that information may be transmitted to and processed by backend workflows and AI-related services used by RWE Americas or its service providers.

Users are informed in the App that conversations and related content may be collected and used strictly to provide troubleshooting responses, session management, and AI-supported functionality.

4. How Information Is Shared

We may share information in the following circumstances:

  • Service providers and processors: with vendors or systems that host, process, transmit, analyze, or support the App and its AI-assisted functionality.
  • Within RWE Americas: with authorized personnel who support operations, maintenance, troubleshooting quality, internal compliance, or security.
  • Legal and safety reasons: if required to comply with applicable law, regulation, legal process, or to protect rights, safety, systems, or users.
  • Business transfers: if relevant in connection with a merger, reorganization, sale, or transfer of assets.

We do not sell personal information through TurbinePeek, and we do not use collected data for third-party advertising or cross-app tracking.

5. Data Retention

We retain information for as long as reasonably necessary to operate the App, support troubleshooting workflows, maintain records, ensure service reliability, meet legal or compliance obligations, resolve disputes, and enforce our agreements.

Retention periods may vary depending on the type of data, operational needs, and applicable legal or company requirements.

6. Security

We use reasonable administrative, technical, and organizational measures intended to protect information processed through the App. However, no method of transmission over the internet or electronic storage is completely secure, and we cannot guarantee absolute security.

7. Your Choices

  • Photos: you choose whether to attach images to a troubleshooting conversation.
  • Nickname/Alias: you choose the nickname or alias entered into the App.
  • Notifications: you can manage local notification permissions in your device settings.

If you would like to request access, correction, or deletion of information associated with your use of TurbinePeek, contact us at guillermo.lozano@rwe.com. We will review requests in accordance with applicable law and operational requirements.

8. Children’s Privacy

TurbinePeek is intended for professional users and is not directed to children. We do not knowingly design the App for use by children.

9. International Processing

Information processed through TurbinePeek may be handled in jurisdictions other than your own, subject to applicable legal, contractual, and organizational safeguards.

10. Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. If we make material changes, we may update the effective date above and take other appropriate steps consistent with applicable requirements.
