TurbinePeek Support

Last updated: March 17, 2026

TurbinePeek is a field troubleshooting assistant for wind turbine technicians and support personnel. This page provides basic support information for the TurbinePeek iOS beta.

1. What TurbinePeek Does

TurbinePeek helps users:

  • start structured troubleshooting sessions,
  • exchange chat messages with the assistant,
  • attach images to support troubleshooting,
  • save, reopen, and continue sessions,
  • resolve sessions with notes,
  • view personal stats and gamification progress.

2. First-Time Setup

When you open the app for the first time:

  • accept the in-app disclaimer about AI usage and conversation collection,
  • enter a company UI in the format UI + 6 digits,
  • enter a nickname or alias for how Gee should address you.
For this beta, the app accepts any company UI value that matches the expected format.

4. Common Topics

  • Can I use the app without a password? Yes. There is no traditional account sign-in.
  • Why does the app ask for a nickname? The nickname is used as a display name for how Gee addresses you in the app.
  • Why does the app ask for notification permission? The app uses local reminders for open troubleshooting sessions.
  • Can I attach photos? Yes. You can optionally attach photos to troubleshooting conversations.
